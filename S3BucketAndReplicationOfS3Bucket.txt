1)S3 Bucket
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) S3 Bucket is noting but a simple storage bucket.

2) By default one bucket provide you 5TB space. you can create 100 buckets.

3) There are different types of bucket such as standard, glassior,
   life cycle or etc.

4) glassior means if you want to store the data and not required often, like
   suppose in year,two year or may be after some month, will put into glassior
   bucket , cost is less but we required data it's not immediately available 
   for you, first you need to raise reqest for data.

5) life cycle s3 bucket means after sometime of you need to specify the days
     data clear get cleared ,like after 50 days or etc.

6) S3 bucket get accesed from any regions.


2) S3 Bucket replication
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1) For S3 bucket replication we required min two buckets, source and destincation
   bucket.

2) Suppose one bucket is in mumbai region and another one Nvirginia regions.
   i will put data into mumbai region, same data get replcated Nvirginia region.
